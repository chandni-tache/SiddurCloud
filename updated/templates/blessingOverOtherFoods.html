All foods that do not grow from the ground get the blessing "shehakol." This includes animal products: meat, chicken, fish, and eggs; water and all other drinks (except for wine); and miscellaneous foods like mushrooms, candy, etc.

After eating foods that are Shehakol, say Borei Nefashot, the After-Blessing After Other Foods.

Blessing Over Other Foods:

Blessed are You, L-rd our G‑d, King of the universe, by Whose word all things came to be.
Baruch atah A-donay, Elo-heinu Melech Ha’Olam shehakol nihiyah bed'varo.

HISTORY OF SIDDUR CLOUD

Siddur Cloud started out as an idea that quickly became a flame between a group of friends that wanted to immensely learn to read and pray in Hebrew. We found that it became increasingly evident that people in our community and elsewhere have found more freedom with the power of the internet - freedom to pray, congregate, celebrate and assemble. We have all this newfound freedom but still, a gap exists between those that were beneficiaries of Yeshiva education and or learned to pray through family, however, those that are just now starting out and finding their true Jewish roots have been disenfranchised by for business. 

This application is meant to serve as a bridge the gap of the aspiring Jew. It has, through an easy to read layout, the English transliterated Hebrew text to start praying at your own level and pace. 

The application will remain free to use forever!

If even one person finds this application useful, the work put in will have been worth it!

Tell your friends and family about us and enjoy. Any feedback is welcome via admin@siddurcloud.com
